Hands-on traning environment

While using EduStance Cloud you will experience a fully-featured hands-on virtual training environment. You will be provided with your own learning environment to run your lab, where you will be able to develop your skills with our instructors, remote labs and a powerful user interface.

Our aim is to provide you all the necessary tools to achieve your goals and succeed.

From anywhere

Enable anyone to train on your solution from anywhere with just a browser and an internet connection, coupled with high quality training courseware.

How does it work?

What kind of training do you need?
We provide you a keys-in-hand solution which integrates the full process of a training.

Training as a service

Edustance enables any business to provide bespoke training on their products to either customers or internal staff, without the need of an in house training team or a hardware infrastructure. You don't have to worry any longer about the maintenance of your solution.

Fitted solution for everybody

You let us know what your training needs are, like training courseware, remote labs, or an isolated environment composed of virtual machines. We will give you a solution strictly tailored to your needs.

Custom your courseware

Give your team all the infrastructure and materials they need to train on your solutions or allow external users (customers, partners) to do the same and certify on your solution. Also our team of experts will work closely with your technical team to create the materials.
